Path: Fridge → Oven/Microwave → Sink → Prep (Wall C) → Hob. Entrance is on the South wall.

Structural Beam Management

The 0.35m ceiling beam prevents wall cabinets reaching full 2.85m height.

  • Install 60 cm or 80 cm high wall cabinets starting at 1.50m from floor level
  • Use UTRUSTA LED lighting strips underneath cabinets
Countertop & Plinth
  • Worktop: KLYKET (Quartz) or custom-cut white quartz with 1.5 cm overhang
  • Plinth: METOD 8 cm plinth in high-gloss white
  • Use the clip-on system for easy cleaning access
