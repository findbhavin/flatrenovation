Structural Beam Management

The 0.35m ceiling beam prevents wall cabinets reaching full 2.85m height.

  • Install 80 cm high wall cabinets (ME 310/3002/335) starting at 1.50m from floor, top at 2.30m
  • MITTLED LED lighting strips under each wall cabinet (80 cm strips)
Countertop & Plinth
  • Worktop: EXHULT custom-cut quartz (Art. 004.510.35) with 1.5 cm overhang
  • Plinth: FÖRBÄTTRA 8 cm plinth, white (5× 220 cm pcs)
  • Legs: METOD 8 cm legs (16 pcs)
